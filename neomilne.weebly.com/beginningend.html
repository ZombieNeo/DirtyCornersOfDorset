beginning/end

THEME

1. Natural World
All living things have a life cycle. After a sensational display of colour and form, flowers lose their petals and seed heads begin to form. In the photograph 'Single Oriental Poppy' by Irving Penn the last two petals are about to drop. Is something more beautiful at the beginning of its life or at the end?

2. Objects
Objects can be used to symbolise life stories from the beginning to the end. a still life artwork which includes various symbolic objects designed to remind the viewer of their mortality and the worthlessness of worldly goods and pleasures could encourage you to think about objects that are precious to you.

Harmen Steenwyck

Abelardo Morell

Money is on the minds of the rich, poor and everybody in the middle and so I find it an interesting subject. Its allure usually has dark undertones but that’s not what interests me.
In my pictures I like concentrating on the elemental fact that money is paper and metal. While it’s hard to separate money’s physical properties from what it symbolically represents, I have tried to make its “thingness” be important. Money as a plaything is also an aspect that figures in this work.
